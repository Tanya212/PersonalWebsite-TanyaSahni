Cooking


Cooking is like painting or writing a song. It is the best hobby to have, because it can be very useful in life. Just as there are only so many notes or colors, there are only so many flavors—it’s how you combine them that sets you apart. I love cooking for my family and friends.

Fashion


Fashion; to me it is an art. It is a concept that can be interpreted in various ways for each individual, which, for me makes it fascinating- everyone has a different style. For some people, self-expression is difficult, having an individual style gives these people the ability to show their personality and beliefs.

Photography

I found my photography constantly growing and adapting. Whatever it is, I find it always stimulates something within my head, the urge to capture and portray the world as I see it...
